Tell SCDOT To LEAVE ANDERSON MILL ROAD ALONE

In Fall 2025, the SC Department of Transportation will update the intersection of Reidville Road and Anderson Mill Road to remove all left turns from the intersection

This will include constructing a new road between 10 Star Fitness and First Horizon Bank to facilitate the new traffic flow

Despite the SCDOT’s claims that this will facilitate smoother traffic flow, not only will the new updates fail to alleviate existing traffic problems, they will cause unnecessary strain on the immediately surrounding businesses and neighborhoods. Drivers will have a harder time accessing the establishments at this intersection, and will be much more likely to cut through the surrounding neighborhoods to avoid the new, much more complicated intersection.
